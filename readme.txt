Customizer plugin for the HeronThemes WordPress themes.

== Description ==

### Create the website you want

With the Heron Customizer Plugin, you can adjust and create the WordPress theme you want. Although our premium themes are gorgeous, there is no need to stop dreaming. 
Start designing and think about all the possibilities out there.

#### Customize all you want

* Easily change colors
* Pick the font you want
* Transform elements on the fly
* Decide which section you want

#### We love KISS (Keep It Stupid Simple)
You don't have to be a Picasso or designer to create a stunning website. The simplicity of the Heron Customizer will blow you away. Just a switch or drag and drop. That's it, nothing more.

### Premium support

We do not always provide active support for the Heron Customizer plugin on the WordPress.org forums, as we prioritize our email support. One-on-one email support is available to people who [bought one of our Premium Themes Premium](https://heronthemes.com/wordpress-themes) only.

== Installation ==

=== From within WordPress ===

1. Visit 'Plugins > Add New'
1. Search for 'Heron Customizer'
1. Activate Heron Customizer from your Plugins page.
1. Go to "after activation" below.

=== Manually ===

1. Upload the `heron-customizer` folder to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
1. Activate the Heron Customizer plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
1. Go to "after activation" below.

=== After activation ===

1. You should see the name of you premium theme in the menu sidebar.
1. If you click on it, the Customizer apears and you are ready to customize your theme.
1. You're done!
